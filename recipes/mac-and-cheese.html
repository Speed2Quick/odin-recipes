Mac and Cheese

Description

Mac and Cheese is a delectable dish comprised of soft pasta and creamy cheese sauce.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 cups of shredded Cheddar cheese
  • ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Add water to a pot and lightly season with salt.
  2. Bring the water to a boil and add the macaroni.
  3. Cook for around 8 minutes.
  4. Melt the butter in a sauce pan over medium heat.
  5. Add flour, pepper, and salt to the butter and mix until smooth.
  6. Slowly add the milk while stirring continuously for around 5 minutes or until smooth.
  7. Add in the cheddar cheese and stir until its melted.
  8. Drain the macaroni and fold them into the sauce.
