Fried Chicken

Description

Fried Chicken is a savory and crunchy dish, which can be made with just a few ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken cut into pieces
  • 3/4 to 1 cup of buttermilk
  • Oil for frying
  • 1 and 1/2 to 2 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1 table spoon of paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground sage
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground thyme
  • 1/8 teaspoon baking powder

Steps

  1. Soak the chicken in buttermilk and refrigerate them for at least an hour.
  2. Add all of the dry ingredients to a bag and shake to combine.
  3. Place a piece of chicken into the bag with the flour mixture, and shake the bag until the chicken is coated.
  4. Place the chicken onto wax paper and repeat for the remaining pieces.
  5. Let the chicken rest for about 15 minutes.
  6. Fill a deep pan with 1/2 an inch of oil.
  7. Place the pan over medium heat until the oil reaches 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  8. Carefully fry your chicken, 2-3 pieces at a time, for 7-8 minutes per side.
  9. Place the freshly fried chicken onto papers towels to dry.
  10. Allow your oil to return to 350 degrees before frying your next batch.
  11. Let your chicken rest for a few minutes and then serve.
