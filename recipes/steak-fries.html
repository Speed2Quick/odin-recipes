Steak Fries

Description

Steak fries are a larger, thicker cut of a regular french fry, that are often more filling.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium russet potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of grill seasoning
  • 1 serving of cooking spray

Steps

  1. Thoroughly scrub your potatoes clean.
  2. Cut each potatoe into eigths.
  3. Preheat an oven to 500 degrees Fahrenheit.
  4. Mix together the olive oil and seasoning in a bowl.
  5. Add the potatoe wedges and coat them by tossing together.
  6. Line 2 baking sheets with lightly crumpled aluminum foil.
  7. Add the wedges onto the pans so that they are evenly spaced out from each other.
  8. Bake in the oven for about 12 minutes and flip, cooking for another 13 minutes or until golden brown.
